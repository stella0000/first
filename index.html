5 Characters Who Have Gotten Me Through Quarantine

It's been an isolating time, but I've found connection and happiness in a few characters. Netflix and Hulu and an unnamed illegal site (I'm kidding, for legal reasons), I thank you for introducing me to these 5 heroes.

Aang

Avatar: The Last Airbender

Aang is so special. Why?

  1. He is the only surviving airbender.
  2. He chooses not to kill the Firelord because it just doesn't sit right with him.
  3. He is over 100 years old, but does not look a day over 12!

I am so proud of him.
